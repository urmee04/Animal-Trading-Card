Golden Pheasant

Golden pheasants lay 8 to 12 eggs at a time and will then incubate these for around 22-23 days.

  • Scientific Name: Chrysolophus pictus
  • Average Length: 90-105 centimeters
  • Average Lifespan: 5-6 years
  • Habitat: Dense forests, woodland and sparse undergrowth
